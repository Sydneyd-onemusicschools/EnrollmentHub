Enrollment Script

Introduction Script

Start with a warm, friendly greeting:

"Hi! Welcome to [School Name]! My name is [Your Name], and I'm so excited to meet you and [Child's Name] today. Thank you for taking the time to visit us!"

Build rapport by asking about their family:

"Before we tour the facility, I'd love to learn a little more about [Child's Name]. What are some of their favorite activities? What are you looking for in a school program?"

Key tip: Listen actively and take mental notes. Reference these details throughout your conversation to show you're paying attention.

Remember: Parents are choosing a place for their most precious gift. Show them you understand this responsibility.
